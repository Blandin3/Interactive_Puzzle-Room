# Puzzle Room - Interactive Environment Challenge

## ðŸŽ® Game Concept

A first-person puzzle game where players are trapped in a locked room and must solve 5 sequential puzzles to escape. Each completed puzzle reveals a cryptic clue for the next challenge. The room progressively dims as puzzles are solved, creating an immersive atmosphere. Players must use observation, logic, and timing to unlock the door and escape.

---

## ðŸ§© Puzzle Tasks

1. **The Switch** - *"Something nearby still remembers how to wake the darkness"*
   - Walk through the switch to activate it
   - Switch turns green and wall lights illuminate the room

2. **The Battery Collection** - *"Scattered energy must be reclaimed before light can endure"*
   - Collect 3 batteries scattered around the room
   - Only available after activating the switch
   - Each battery disappears when collected

3. **The Star** - *"With power restored, something begins to shine above"*
   - Find and collect the glowing star
   - Star disappears with a sound effect when collected

4. **The Pressure Plates** - *"The floor remembers every stepâ€”not all paths forgive mistakes"*
   - Jump over 3 pressure plates in sequence
   - Plates change color when activated
   - All plates disappear when the 3rd plate is reached

5. **The Pillars** - *"Only harmony opens the way; let all face the same truth"*
   - Walk into pillars to rotate them 90 degrees
   - Align all pillars to the same rotation
   - Door unlocks when all pillars are aligned

## ðŸŽ® Controls

### Movement
- **W** - Move Forward
- **A** - Move Left
- **S** - Move Backward
- **D** - Move Right
- **Mouse** - Look Around
- **Space** - Jump
- **ESC** - Unlock Cursor

### Interaction
- **E** - Interact with objects (switch)
- **Walk Through** - Collect items (batteries, star)
- **Walk Into** - Activate objects (pillars, plates)

### Gameplay Tips
- Move speed: 18 units/second
- Jump while moving forward (Space + W) to clear pressure plates
- Puzzles must be completed in order
- Watch for cryptic clues in the UI
